BASF results reveal improving demand NEWS DIGEST
BASF , the world 's largest chemicals company by sales , provided further evidence that demand from the sector 's many customer industries remains solid enough to help many chemicals companies compensate for rising raw materials prices .
BASF topped expectations with second-quarter results , and raised its outlook for the full year .
It expects " significantly " higher sales and an increase in earnings before interest and tax before special items in 2005 .
The improved outlook follows a 14 per cent rise in sales to Euros 10.6bn ( Dollars 12.9bn ) during the second quarter , as earnings before interest , taxes and special items jumped one-third to Euros 1.7bn.
Bettina Wassener , Frankfurt
