Barclays bids for ING 's French arm NEWS DIGEST
Barclays has made an offer to buy the wealth management business of ING in France for an undisclosed sum .
The UK 's third-largest bank is to acquire ING Ferri and ING Private Banking , subject to consultations with employees .
The acquisition will create an operation with 50 branches , 140,000 clients and Euros 10bn ( Pounds 7bn ) of assets under management .
It will increase the number of Barclays ' customers in France by 25 per cent .
Barclays has been looking to increase the proportion of profit that it generates from outside the UK .
About 20 per cent of the bank 's Pounds 3.85bn annual profit comes from outside the UK .
